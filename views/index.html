Get ready for the next AGM!


Another year has gone by and our club has grown another year wiser. Believe it or not, MASAT is already 6 years old! And it's that time of the year again where the current committee members will pass the baton to new hands. This year, the Annual General Meeting (AGM) will be held on April 2nd, 2015.

This website will advertise candidate profiles and manifesto. It it also built with popular vote poll where you can vote and see current popular vote results. Our important feature is Manifesto upvote where you can endorse any manifesto you like!
